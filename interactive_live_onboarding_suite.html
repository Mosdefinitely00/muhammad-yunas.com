Onboarding Bottleneck Mitigation

Bottleneck 1: Product-to-Revenue Sync Drag

The Risk: Delayed communication loops between product development and product marketing slow launch cycles.

Mitigation: Establish a continuous, weekly sync pipeline between PM and PMM to lock messaging alignments early.

Bottleneck 2: Sales Field Adoption Resistance

The Risk: Outbound commercial teams defaulting back to familiar, feature-heavy presentation decks.

Mitigation: Treat Sales as our internal customer. Run dynamic bootcamps and co-sell on live strategic deals to prove the model.

Bottleneck 3: Technical-Regulatory Complexity

The Risk: Messaging getting lost in complex tax compliance or technical product jargon, stalling out sales cycles.

Mitigation: Translate regulatory specifications directly into CFO value frameworks while leaving details to backend documentation.
